About this venue

Experience professional-grade gameplay at the Downtown Arena. Our Pitch A features the latest generation FIFA-certified synthetic turf, perfect for fast-paced 5-a-side matches. The arena is equipped with high-lumen LED floodlights for excellent visibility during evening games. Changing rooms with hot showers are available on-site.
